Who We Are

Established in 2021, We are CatsRUs based in Toronto Ontario and we specialize in providing new cat owners with brand options for food, supplies, and toys. We have a strong team that is dedicated to researching about new and innovative cat brands. This could be a new high-quality cat food all the way to a brand new innovative cat tree that implements many features. This website will be used for our research team who update our shop page regularly with new additions to our categories. So far, we are displaying a variety of different cat food, supplies, and toys each with their own unique pro's list. For example we recently showcased Nature's Variety® Instinct® which is a very innovative brand that utilizes organ meat for protein. We also make sure to only feature products that pose good reviews from other consumers so you, the new feline owner can choose from a variety of starting places for each category of product. Our CEO of CatsRUs, Maneesh Wijewardhana, has many feline friends and has said that it was intimidating when he got his first cat. Since he was new to it, he didn't know which brands to choose from and what supplies were truly important and had to resort to trial and error. Now you can see where the inspiration came from when it comes to CatRUs. This website is still in development and we take suggestions from our users when it comes to categories and brands. We hope to open up a physical store in the near future which will store inhouse products and also external brands/sponsors. We hope to have these companies that we showcase reach out to us in the near future so we can display them physically because some people rather see it in person. Remember, if you're gonna shop for cat products, remember, CatsRUs!

Our Mission

Our mission statement from the beginning was always to listen to the customer. We understand how intimidating being a new cat owner or soon to be owner is. What cat food is the best? Do I even need a cat tree? What type of toys will my cat enjoy playing with? These are all questions that our CEO had when he was once a first cat owner and with that, this inspiration began. We will always cater to the new audiences but that doesn't mean that veteran owners can't get a benefit out of us. We regularly update our site with new and innovative cat products whether that be food, supplies, or toys. This means that if you've been rinse and repeating the same things for your kitty, maybe its time for a change? We deeply care about cats well-being and so we know that some cats get unhappy without change. Maybe the food you've been giving them is lacking protein or the tree that they scratch and sleep in is getting ruined overtime. With CatsRUs, there is always a product or brand that is new for someone. This is what we strive for and try to achieve. We want CatsRUs to be known as the company that cares about giving your feline friend, the best you can give them. This also means staying within budget but also staying within quality. Another mission statement with us is customer transparency and feedback. We want you to know that we are always open to new ideas, new additions, and new suggestions from our beloved users. Just email us CatsRUs@catmail.com what your suggestion is. This could be a new category of product, a new cat food brand, or a wacky toy that's never before seen. Let us know!
